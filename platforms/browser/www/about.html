About

Rubik's Cube is a 3D combination puzzle. Originally invented in 1974 by Erno Rubik, quickly became one of the best-selling toys all over the world. Despite being the most popular in 1980s, lately it is experiencing its renaissance, gathering many people who call themselves speedcubers.
